What I do: I help students overcome their fears of the dreaded three-letter tests (SAT, ACT, GRE) and write standout essays that get them into great universities. I’ve tutored hundreds of students since 2013, using a teaching process focused on building reasoning and critical thinking skills that will help them for the rest of their lives.


My track record: Students I've worked with have been accepted to many excellent colleges and universities: Boston University, Bryn Mawr, Bucknell, Case Western, Cornell, Drexel, Harvard, Lehigh, MIT, NYU, Penn State, Pitt, Princeton, RISD, Rutgers, TCNJ, UC Boulder, UC San Diego, University of Chicago, Wesleyan, William & Mary, and Villanova (and many more). I've been incredibly lucky to work with so many talented students!


College essays: College essays are unlike most writing that high school students are used to. They're basically like telling a story about yourself. Fun fact: I once wrote about my adventures working at Burger King for a college essay. I'll help you spotlight YOUR story, making it unforgettable.
